Main Axis / Cross Axis

ROW: Main = X axis | Cross = Y Axis

COL: Main = Y axis | Cross = X Axis

justify-content: (Move content across Main Axis)
flex-start; (Default)
flex-end; (End of Axis)
center; (Center of axis)
space-evenly; (Seperate Evenly)
space-between; (Pushed to edge, lots of space between)
space-around; (1/2 space on edge. full space in middle)

align-items: (Move content across Cross Axis
flex-start; (Default)
flex-end; (End of Axis)
center; (center of Axis)
baseline; ()

Flex-wrap: (If content is too large, it wraps onto next line (or not)) wrap; (enables wrapping)
nowrap; (disables)

align-content: (align multiple lines of content)
flex-start; (Default)
flex-end; (End of Axis)
center; (Center of axis)
space-between; (Pushed to edge, lots of space between)
space-around; (1/2 space on edge. full space in middle)

align-self: (align single flex-item that overrides align-items)
flex-start; (Default)
flex-end; (End of Axis)
center; (Center of axis)
auto; (automatically)
baseline; ()

Flex Item Properties

flex-basis: Sets initial size of a flex item (Row=Width / Col=Height)
flex-grow: Sets growth factor (0 = disabled / 1 = enabled / 2 = grow factor 2)
flex-shrink:Sets shrink factor (0 = disabled / 1 = enabled / 2 = shrink factor 2)
shorthand flex: Does it all! (value 1 = grow / value 2 = shrink or basis / Value 3 = basis)
