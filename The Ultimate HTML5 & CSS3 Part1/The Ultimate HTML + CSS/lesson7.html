HTML/7

Lists;

Are represented within HTML by;
ul(Unordered List) or ol(Ordered List), and li(List Item) tags.

The ul tag, is used for unordered Lists, like a list of ingredients
The ol tag is used for ordered Lists, like steps of a recepie
Regardless of the type of list, it needs li List Item tags to hold the data

Important Tip; To hide the bullet points / numbers of the list
in CSS its list-style-type: none;

<ol> or <ul>
<li > One</li >
<li > Two</li >
<li > Three</li >
</ol > or </ul>
