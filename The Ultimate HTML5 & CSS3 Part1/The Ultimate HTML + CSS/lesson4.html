Links;

Links are represented by <a> tags. (Short for Anchor)
There are two types of Link; Absolute and Relative

Absolute links, go to an absolutely new place on the internet
<a href="https://gooogle.co.uk"> Click Me </a>
Gives us... Click Me

Relative links, go to a place in the same file structure
(relative to the current page.)
<a href="index.html"> Take me home!</a>
Gives us ...

Take me home!
