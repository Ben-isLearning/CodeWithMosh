Forbidden Characters / Entities

There are some forbidden characters within HTML,
because they mess with funcionality
Obvously, we cant just not use these characters...
We have stand-ins, called Entities.

Entities begin with & and end with ;
Some examples of these (without the lines)
&-lt-; gives us <
&-gt-; gives us >
&-copy-; gives us ©
&-nbsp-; gives us a Non-Breaking Space
